How to Play

Objective

A social deduction game where all players but one (the "Imposter") know a secret word. The Imposter must blend in while others try to identify them.

Game Setup

• 4-12 players take turns using one phone
• Enter player names and select a category
• One player is randomly chosen as the Imposter

How to Play

• Each player secretly views their role
• Non-imposters see the secret word
• The Imposter only sees the category
• Go around giving one-word clues (2 full rounds)
• Vote to identify the Imposter

Scoring

• Crew wins: Everyone gets 1 point if Imposter is caught
• Imposter wins: Gets 1 point for survival
• Bonus: Imposter gets +1 point for guessing the word correctly
